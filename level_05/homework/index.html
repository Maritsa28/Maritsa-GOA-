Spider-Man

  1. Spider-Man in Comics:

    • Spider-Man is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics.
    • He was created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko.
    • Spider-Man first appeared in the anthology comic book Amazing Fantasy #15 in August 1962 during the Silver Age of Comic Books

  2. Spider-Man in Movies

    • Spider-Man has been featured in several movies over the years.
    • One of the most iconic Spider-Man films is “Spider-Man” (2002), directed by Sam Raimi and starring Tobey Maguire as Peter Parker/Spider-Man
    • The latest Spider-Man film is “Spider-Man: No Way Home”, which was released in 2021. It features Tom Holland as Spider-Man, along with appearances from other characters and villains from different universes
    • You can watch the official trailer for “Spider-Man: No Way Home” on the official YouTube channel

  3. Plot Summary of “Spider-Man: No Way Home

    • In this film, for the first time in cinematic history, Spider-Man’s identity is revealed, causing conflict between his Super Hero responsibilities and his normal life.
    • When he enlists Doctor Strange’s help to restore his secret identity, a spell tears a hole in their world, releasing powerful villains from different universes.
    • Now, Peter Parker must overcome his greatest challenge yet, altering not only his own future but also the future of the Multiverse
